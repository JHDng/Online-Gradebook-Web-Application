title Event: GetDatesStudent

participant Request
participant GetDatesStudent
participant ExamDateDAO
participant Response

Request->GetDatesStudent: doGet(courseID)

GetDatesStudent->ExamDateDAO: findRegisteredDatesByStudentID(studentID, courseID)
ExamDateDAO-->GetDatesStudent: dates || null

alt dates == null || dates.isEmpty
    GetDatesStudent->Response: setStatus(404)
    GetDatesStudent->Response: println("No dates found")
else
    GetDatesStudent->Response: setStatus(200)
    GetDatesStudent->Response: println(json)
else SQLException
    GetDatesStudent->Response: setStatus(500)
    GetDatesStudent->Response: println("Error in finding courses")
end
